yuan

Emoji messaging app.

Basically Yuan app can notify user's current mood, feeling, and where am I. For example, I can share my current mood to girlfriend telling her I'm home, I'm very happy now, I'm sad actually. By just a few simple clicks on the app, you can send a Smiley or emoji to your friend, your family, or your love ones. You not only can send to a person but also can broadcast your message to a group of people you have created. ie I'm starving to your group of best friends so some of them may quickly pick up a phone to call you up for a dinner.

Besides, the app also capable to show where are your location now if you are willing to share out. For more advance, the app able to auto update your current activity what you are doing. Like the pedeometer sensor should know that whether you are currently driving in a car, or jogging in a park. The app also can notify your friends what song you're playing right now with your phone.
